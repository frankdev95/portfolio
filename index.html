About

I am a predominantly self taught full-stack developer from Kent, England. I have been exploring the vast world of programming for four years, and during that time have inquisitively delved into numerous fields. I was introduced to programming during my music degree, which immediately ignited a strong, sincere passion and desire to learn more. Since then, I have studied numerous programming fields such as game, app, web and software development. During my studies I have acquired a vast portfolio of which a portion I showcase in this website. My strong ambition is to cement my place on the career ladder, applying my skills and knowledge in a professional setting, and to strengthen my abilities as a programmer.
